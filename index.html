I'm Fábio

I'm a mechanical engineer who found out during university that programming is my passion.

From there I took any programming related subject I could pick, delving into PLCs, robotics, computer vision, microcontrollers and later to high level languages like C. During my master's I explored front-end as well, mainly HTML5, CSS3 and the Vue.Js framework.

After university I realized I lacked a lot of knowledge not only of more recent programming languages (C#, Python) but also of good software design practices and even some basic CS concepts like OOP, data structures or the big O notation.

Thus, for the past months I have been educating myself on these matters, as well as methodologies like Agile and DevOps.
